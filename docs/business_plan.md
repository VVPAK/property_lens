# Property Lens - Business Plan

## Concept
**AI-Platform for Dubai Real Estate (iOS + Android/PWA)**
Focus: Investment, buying, and renting properties using Artificial Intelligence.

## Goal
Create a mobile AI platform that:
- Helps investors and buyers make fast, safe decisions.
- Automates selection, analysis, ROI calculation, and transactions.
- Combines Rent + Buy in one app.
- Acts as a Marketplace + AI Consultant.

## Target Audience
- **Segments**:
    - Investors from CIS (Commonwealth of Independent States).
    - Investors and buyers from UAE.
    - Private buyers (First home, Buy-to-Let).
- **Needs**:
    - Avoid communicating with 10+ different brokers.
    - Want clear numbers, ROI, and risk analysis.

## Market Segment (Dubai)
- **Rent**: 70,000 – 80,000 AED / year
- **Buy**: Studios / 1BR (700,000 – 1,200,000 AED)
- **Characteristics**: Mass market, liquid, understandable for investment.

## AI Features (The Core Value)
- **Search**: By goal (Investment, Living, Renting).
- **Calculation**: ROI, Cash Flow, Payback period.
- **Analysis**: Neighborhoods, Developers, Liquidity, Risks.
- **Consultant**: 24/7 AI Broker chat.
- **Forecasting**: Price growth, Rental yield predictions.

## Platform Format
- **Mobile First**: PWA (Next.js) initially, transitioning to Native (React Native).
- **AI Assistant**: Inside the app (Chat).
- **UX**: Minimum manual input, maximum automation ("Tinder for Homes").

## Monetization Model
- Commission from Sales (Sharing with brokers).
- Commission from Rentals.
- Future: Premium access, Paid analytics, Investor subscriptions.

## Competitive Advantage
- **No**: Peer pressure from brokers, Chaos, Unwanted calls.
- **Yes**: Numbers, Logic, Transparency, Trust.
